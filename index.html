About Us


Clavius A is a Grade 11 STEM section at Xavier University - Ateneo de Cagayan Senior High School. Their section priest is Father Christopher Clavius, a Jesuit German mathematician and astronomer who was a member of the Vatican commission that accepted the proposed calendar invented by Aloysius Lilius, that is known as Gregorian calendar.
